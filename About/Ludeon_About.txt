[center][size=20pt]Gasch's Gear - Guns and Grenades[/size][/center]
Target Version: 0.19.2

The aim of Gasch's Gear mods is to add small amounts of content and rebalancing to produce a smoother progression curve from tribal to spacer and make lesser-used portions of the game more meaningful.
  
[color=orange][b]Overview[/b][/color]
[list]
[li]More early-era firearms![/li]
[li]More distinct firearms across all ages![/li]
[li]Grenades are dangerous![/li]
[li]Firearm and Grenade research completely overhauled![/li]
[li]All ranged weapons are rebalanced![/li]
[/list]

[color=orange][b]Compatibility[/b][/color]
This is very likely to conflict with mods which alter vanilla firearms or firearm related research. It will not cause a crash if added to unmodded saves, but will cause many vanilla firearms and molotovs to disappear.
